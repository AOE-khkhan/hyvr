HyVR ExampleÂ¶

Two examples are provided with the HyVR simulation module: the MADE site example made.ini), mimicking the MADE site in Columbus, Mississippi, and a test case with user-defined architectural element mean contact surface elevations (test_lu.ini).

The MADE site example is described in more deatil in the following article in Groundwater: Bennett, J. P., Haslauer, C. P., Ross, M., & Cirpka, O. A. (2018). An open, object-based framework for generating anisotropy in sedimentary subsurface models. Groundwater. DOI:10.1111/gwat.12803. A preprint version of the article is available here.

Example field mimicking the MADE site, generated using HyVR.
